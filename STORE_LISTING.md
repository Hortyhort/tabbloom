# TabBloom - Chrome Web Store Listing

## Name
TabBloom - Your Digital Garden

## Short Description (132 characters max)
Turn your browser tabs into a living garden. Watch flowers bloom, wilt, and grow based on your browsing habits.

## Detailed Description

**Transform your tab chaos into a serene digital sanctuary.**

TabBloom turns every browser tab into a beautiful oriental lily in your personal garden. Active tabs bloom vibrantly, while neglected ones slowly wilt — giving you a gentle, visual reminder to tend to your browsing.

### Features

**Living Garden Visualization**
- Each tab becomes a unique flower in your garden
- Active tabs bloom with vibrant petals
- Neglected tabs gradually wilt over time
- Harvest wilted tabs to earn coins and keep your garden tidy

**Seasonal Magic**
- Spring: Extra butterflies flutter through soft pastel flowers
- Summer: Warm sun rays pulse gently across your garden
- Autumn: Orange leaves drift down among earth-toned blooms
- Winter: Soft snowflakes fall in a peaceful, quiet garden

**Cozy ASMR Experience**
- Gentle ambient soundscapes for each season
- Soft chimes when harvesting flowers
- Delicate wing-flutter sounds when butterflies land
- All sounds are subtle and relaxing

**Satisfying Interactions**
- Flowers lift and glow when you hover
- Beautiful particle bursts when harvesting
- Smooth animations throughout
- One-click "Harvest Dormant Tabs" to clean up

**Share Your Garden**
- Capture beautiful screenshots of your garden
- Privacy options to blur tab titles
- Share your cozy sanctuary with friends

**Dark Mode Support**
- Automatically adapts to your system theme
- Night garden with muted, relaxing colors

### Why TabBloom?

In a world of productivity apps that stress you out, TabBloom takes a different approach. It doesn't judge your tab hoarding — it turns it into something beautiful.

Check in on your garden, harvest what you don't need, and watch your digital space bloom.

# Icon Guidelines

## Design Concept
A blooming oriental lily emerging from a browser tab, with warm sunset gradient background.

## Color Palette
- Primary: #5D7A4A (warm green)
- Accent: #FFB7C5 (soft pink petals)
- Background gradient: #7BA4D4 → #F8C9A8 → #FFE4D6 (sunset)

## Icon Sizes Needed
- 16x16 px (toolbar)
- 48x48 px (extensions page)
- 128x128 px (store listing)

## Icon Elements
1. Simplified lily flower (3-4 petals visible)
2. Small tab/window shape as the "pot" or base
3. Subtle sparkle or glow effect
4. Rounded corners for friendly feel
