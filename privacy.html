TabBloom Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary: TabBloom stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information or browsing data.

What Data TabBloom Accesses

TabBloom requires the "tabs" permission to function. This allows the extension to:

  • See which tabs are open in your browser
  • Track when you last visited each tab
  • Close tabs when you harvest them

What Data TabBloom Stores

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API:

  • Tab activity timestamps: When each tab was last active (stored locally)
  • Garden statistics: Total harvests, coins earned, streaks (stored locally)
  • User preferences: Sound settings, animation preferences, garden name (synced to your Chrome account if sync is enabled)

What TabBloom Does NOT Do

  • We do NOT collect your browsing history
  • We do NOT record URLs or page content
  • We do NOT transmit any data to external servers
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • We do NOT store tab titles or URLs beyond the current session

Third-Party Libraries

TabBloom includes the following open-source libraries (bundled locally, no external requests):

  • html2canvas: Used only when you choose to screenshot your garden
  • canvas-confetti: Used for celebration animations

These libraries run entirely in your browser and do not transmit any data. No external CDN requests are made.

Data Retention

  • Tab activity data is automatically cleaned up when tabs are closed
  • You can reset all statistics at any time from the Settings page
  • You can export your data from Settings for personal records
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View your statistics in the garden stats popup
  • Export: Download your data as JSON from Settings
  • Delete: Reset all stats from Settings, or uninstall the extension

Children's Privacy

TabBloom does not knowingly collect any personal information. The extension is suitable for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.

In plain English: TabBloom is a fun, local-only extension. Your browsing stays private. We don't want your data - we just want you to enjoy watching your tabs bloom.
